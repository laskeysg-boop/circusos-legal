CircusOS

A private, single-user personal assistant.

CircusOS is personal-use software operated by its owner to organize daily life — tasks, schedules, notes, and personal health summaries. It is not a public service and has no other users.

With the owner's explicit authorization, CircusOS may read the owner's own wellness data (such as Oura daily readiness, sleep, and activity summaries) to inform personal briefings. That data stays on a single personal device and is never sold or shared with third parties.
