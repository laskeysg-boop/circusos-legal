Privacy Policy

CircusOS · Last updated June 15, 2026

CircusOS is a private, single-user personal assistant operated by Scott Laskey for personal use only. It is not offered to the public and has no other users. This policy describes how CircusOS handles data accessed through third-party integrations.

Health and wellness data (Oura)

With the owner's explicit OAuth authorization, CircusOS reads the owner's own Oura daily readiness, sleep, and activity summaries. This data is fetched to a single personal device, stored locally with restricted file permissions, and is never sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

Only derived wellness summaries (for example, "readiness: good") are used internally to inform personal briefings. Raw physiological values are not transmitted off the device.

Data sharing

CircusOS does not sell or share personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party. There is no analytics, tracking, or third-party data transfer of integrated wellness data.

Data retention

Data is retained locally only as long as needed for personal use and may be deleted by the owner at any time.

Revoking access

The owner may revoke CircusOS's access to Oura data at any time from the connected-apps settings in the owner's Oura account. Use of the Oura API is subject to the Oura API Agreement.
